Welcome to the Wizarding World

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

The main story arc concerns Harry's conflict with Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic, and subjugate all wizards and Muggles.

Character Bios

Harry Potter

The Boy Who Lived, known for his lightning scar and bravery.

  • Defeated Lord Voldemort as a baby
  • Gryffindor House member
  • Skilled in Defense Against the Dark Arts

Hermione Granger

The brightest witch of her age, known for her intelligence and loyalty.

  • Muggle-born witch
  • Gryffindor House member
  • Exceptional in all subjects

Ron Weasley

Harry best friend, known for his humor and strategic mind.

  • Pure-blood wizard
  • Gryffindor House member
  • Excellent chess player

Albus Dumbledore

Headmaster of Hogwarts, one of the most powerful wizards of all time.

  • Founder of the Order of the Phoenix
  • Defeated dark wizard Grindelwald
  • Master of the Elder Wand

Severus Snape

Potions Master at Hogwarts, with a complex past and allegiances.

  • Former Death Eater turned spy for Dumbledore
  • Head of Slytherin House
  • Skilled in Occlumency and Legilimency

Lord Voldemort

The Dark Lord, the main antagonist of the series.

  • Born as Tom Marvolo Riddle
  • Created seven Horcruxes
  • Feared by most of the wizarding world
